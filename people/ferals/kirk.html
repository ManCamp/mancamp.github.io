World's Worst

After a mysterious past in Biscuitville, World’s Worst joined Man Camp, bringing to the site all the power tools, sandwiches, and perfect drinks even a Texan could desire. However, be warned! His lovable goofiness hides his devious projects that occasionally show up to wave their fiery heads.


Powers:

  • Cowboy Mode Activates When “Deep in the Heart of Texas” Plays
  • Ability to Make Hippies Come and, More Importantly, Go
  • Demonically Possessed Conglomerate of Metal and Bone (Tetanus)

Weaknesses:

  • Strong Gusts of Wind Against His Kilt
  • Texas Sized Heart
