Logic

The token introvert of Man Camp, Logic can be spotted for only brief periods of time before disappearing into a fog of cigarette smoke and sneakily placed one-liners. In the game of life, Logic is the wares seller before a big boss, but be warned! His cooler water is not always what it seems.

Powers:

  • Impervious to Cold
  • Ability to Appear and Disappear at Will
  • Organized in the Midst of Chaos- Seek Answers Here!

Weaknesses:

  • Filthy Humans Trying to Socialize
  • Secretly A Robot- Do Not Put in Water
